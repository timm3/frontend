Welcome to the future of signing up for classes

ClassTable has all of UIUC's current classes in a highly searchable form with the largest collection of class and professor ratings, grades, and reviews.

Use RegNow to grab these classes' elusive open seats while away from the computer. Gone is the task of checking for your classes' statuses every hour between classes on your phone.
